## About the project

For Emprecords we developed a modern webshop where vinyl records can be ordered online.
The focus was on creating a fast shopping experience and an efficient way to automatically synchronize the catalog of vinyl releases.

The webshop was built on Shopify and extended with a custom plugin that retrieves product data from an external source.

### Key improvements

- automatic synchronization of vinyl releases
- fast and clear shopping experience
- optimized product pages
- mobile-friendly webshop
