## About the project

For Bergheuvel we developed an informational website for a construction company
that is establishing its presence in the Netherlands.

The goal of the website is to clearly present the company, explain its services,
and make it easy for potential clients to get in touch.

### Key improvements

- clear structure for services and projects
- professional presentation of the construction company
- fast and mobile-friendly website
- simple way for customers to get in touch
