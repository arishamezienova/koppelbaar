## About the project

For this hair salon we developed an online booking platform
that allows clients to easily schedule their appointments.

Customers can select a service, choose an available time slot,
and automatically receive a confirmation email after completing the booking.
Through the management dashboard, the hairdresser can manage appointments,
modify services and block unavailable time slots.

### Key improvements

- customers can book appointments online
- automatic confirmation email after booking
- clear dashboard for managing appointments
- ability to add services and block unavailable time slots
